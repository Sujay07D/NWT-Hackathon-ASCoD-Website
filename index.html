Roles you can apply for:


President

Leads the committee and oversees all activities.

Sets goals, agendas, and ensures they are met.

Represents the committee in official matters.

Vice-President

Supports the president in decision-making and leadership.

Steps in during the President's absence.

Helps coordinate between different secretaries and teams.

Technical Secretary

Manages the technical aspects of committee operations.

Oversees online platforms and tech-related events.r

Cultural Secretary

Sports Secretary

Committee Volunteer

Supports event planning and execution.

Gains experience in different areas of committee work.
